## Critical Findings

### üö® The FOX Problem

**Every other town outperforms FOX in competitive metrics:**

1. **Win Rate:** All 5 towns win 6-20% more games than FOX
2. **Goal Differential:** All 5 towns have 1.2-5.1 better GD per team
3. **Spring Retention:** All 5 towns retain 8-46% more teams
4. **Offensive Output:** 4 of 5 towns score 7-19% more goals

**FOX is consistently at the bottom despite:**
- Middle-of-pack participation rate (0.78)
- Middle-of-pack competitive division placement (3.08)

This suggests **systemic coaching, player development, or organizational issues** rather than just a small program or weak competition.

### üéØ Other Key Patterns

**Bellingham (BEL):**
- Smallest program (34.7% lower participation than FOX)
- But HIGHEST win rate (55.5%, +19.7% vs FOX)
- Excellent retention (98%, 2nd best)

- HIGHEST offensive output (23.27 goals/team, +19.1% vs FOX)
- Tied for 2nd in win rate (54.3%)

- But lower competitive level (45% in Div 4)
- **Offensive powerhouse in lower divisions**

**Hopkinton (HOP):**
- Largest total program (351 teams)
- 2nd highest participation rate (+7.9% vs FOX)
- But near break-even performance (49.4% win, -0.25 GD)
- Weakest retention after FOX (74.6%)
- Large but struggling to compete

## Recommendations for FOX

Based on this analysis, Foxborough should investigate:

1. **Coaching Quality:** Why is win rate 6-20% below every other town?
